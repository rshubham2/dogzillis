About Us

Dogzilli's was born out of a shared love for reinventing the ordinary and turning it into something extraordinary. The founders, a group of food enthusiasts with diverse culinary backgrounds, came together to redefine the hotdog experience. With a commitment to quality and creativity, we set out to craft hotdogs that not only satisfy your cravings but also leave your taste buds wanting more.
